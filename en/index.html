Frequently asked questions

Common Questions

Where can I find the Bucharest metro map?

The app gives you direct access to routes, station details, next train arrival, nearby places and airport connections. For a simple overview of the network, you can also browse the web map at metroubucuresti.ro/map.html. You can zoom in, search any station and filter lines individually.

How many metro lines does Bucharest have?

Bucharest has 5 active metro lines operated by Metrorex S.A.: M1 (gold), M2 (blue), M3 (red), M4 (green) and M5 (orange), covering approximately 78 km across 63 stations.

Is the Bucharest metro app free?

Yes, completely free to download and use on Android. No in-app purchases, no account required. Works 100% offline.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes — 100% offline. Route planning, schedules, GPS tracking, accessibility info and station details all work underground without mobile data.

Which metro lines does the app cover?

All 5 active Bucharest metro lines: M1 (Dristor–Pantelimon/Preciziei), M2 (Tudor Arghezi–Pipera), M3 (Preciziei–Pantelimon), M4 (Gara de Nord–Strălești), M5 (Eroilor–Valea Cascadelor).

Is this the official Metrorex app?

MetroBuc is an independent app, unaffiliated with Metrorex S.A. It uses publicly available transport data to provide free, offline navigation for passengers.

What are the Bucharest metro operating hours?

Bucharest metro runs daily from approximately 05:00 to 23:00. Exact departure times for every station are available offline in the MetroBuc app — including underground, without signal.

Which metro app works without internet in Bucharest?

MetroBuc works 100% offline — map, routes, schedules, GPS tracking and accessibility info are all stored on your phone. No signal or mobile data needed.

How do I travel between major stations in Bucharest by metro?

Use the MetroBuc route planner: select your departure and destination station, and the app shows the fastest route with all required transfers and estimated time — all offline.

Ready to navigate Bucharest Metro?

Free, offline, no account needed. Works on any Android device — even underground.
