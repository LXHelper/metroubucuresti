02

Description of Service

Metrou București is a free Android application providing:

  • Metro route planning across lines M1–M5 with transfer optimisation
  • Timetable information for all active stations
  • GPS-based route tracking during your journey
  • Station details including entrances, door-opening direction indicators, and transport connections
  • Nearby places and points of interest around each station
  • Personal station notes and favourite routes stored locally on your device
  • Interactive metro map and OpenStreetMap integration
  • Support for 6 languages: English, Romanian, French, Italian, German, Spanish
ℹ️
Informational use only Timetable and route data is compiled from public sources. Always verify critical journey information through official channels.
