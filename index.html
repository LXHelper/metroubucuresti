What it does

Everything you need
underground

FREE to download, works fully OFFLINE and built with PRIVACY first in mind — no accounts needed, no tracking, and no personal data is ever collected by our servers. Crash reports are gathered only with your explicit agreement and consent.

🗺️

Smart Route Planning

Find the best path across M1–M5 with automatic transfer optimisation, door-side recommendations, and journey time estimates.

📍

GPS Route Tracking

Follow your journey step-by-step in real time. Get notified before transfers and when to get off — even with the screen off.

🚪

Door Direction Indicators

Know exactly which side the doors open at every station so you can position yourself before the train arrives.

♿

Accessibility & Station Info

See elevator availability and wheelchair-accessible entrances at every station. Includes bathroom locations, transport connections, and nearby places — all at a glance.

⭐

Favourites & Notes

Save your most-used routes for one-tap navigation and attach personal notes to any station — stored only on your device.

🕐

Train Timetables

Next departures, first and last train times, peak-hour schedules for weekdays and weekends — all fully offline.

🗺️

Interactive Map & Nearby Places

Zoomable metro map with live station markers, entrance pins, elevator locations, and nearby restaurants, parks and landmarks — all overlaid on an interactive OpenStreetMap.

🌙

Theme & Language

Light, dark, or system theme. Switch instantly between English, Romanian, French, Italian, German, and Spanish.

📵

100% Offline — Always

No internet required. Routes, timetables, maps, accessibility info and tracking all work underground without a single byte of mobile data.
