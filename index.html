What it does

Everything you need underground

Built for daily commuters and first-time visitors alike. Every feature works without a server — your data stays on your phone.

🗺️

Smart Route Planning

Find the best path across M1–M5 with automatic transfer optimisation, door-side recommendations, and journey time estimates.

📍

GPS Route Tracking

Follow your journey step-by-step in real time. Get notified before transfers and when to get off — even with the screen off.

🚪

Door Direction Indicators

Know exactly which side the doors open at each station so you can position yourself before the train arrives.

🏢

Station Details

Explore entrances, transport connections, nearby places and points of interest for every station on the network.

⭐

Favourites & Notes

Save your most-used routes for one-tap navigation and attach personal notes to any station — stored only on your device.

🌙

Theme & Language

Light, dark, or system theme. Switch between English, Romanian, French, Italian, German, and Spanish at any time.

🗺️

Interactive Metro Map

Zoomable SVG metro map plus an OpenStreetMap view with station markers, entrance locations, and nearby POI overlays.

🕐

Train Timetables

View next departures, first and last train times, and peak-hour schedules for weekdays and weekends — fully offline.

📵

Works Offline

All route planning, timetables, station data, and journey tracking work without any internet connection — perfect underground.
