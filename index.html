Frequently asked questions

Common Questions

Where can I find the Metrou București map?

The app gives you direct access to routes, station details, next train arrival, nearby places and airport connections. For a simple overview of the network, you can also browse the web map at metroubucuresti.ro/map.html. You can zoom in, search any station and filter lines individually.

How many metro lines does Bucharest have?

Bucharest has 5 active metro lines operated by Metrorex S.A.: M1 (gold), M2 (blue), M3 (red), M4 (green) and M5 (orange), covering approximately 78 km across 63 stations.

Is the Metrou București app free?

Yes, completely free to download and use on Android. No in-app purchases, no account required. Works 100% offline.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes — 100% offline. Route planning, schedules, GPS tracking, accessibility info and station details all work underground without mobile data.

Which metro lines does the app cover?

All 5 active Metrou București lines: M1 (Dristor–Pantelimon/Preciziei), M2 (Tudor Arghezi–Pipera), M3 (Preciziei–Pantelimon), M4 (Gara de Nord–Strălești), M5 (Eroilor–Valea Cascadelor).

Is this the official Metrorex app?

MetrouBUC is an independent app, unaffiliated with Metrorex S.A. It uses publicly available transport data to provide free, offline navigation for passengers.

What are the Metrou București operating hours?

Metrou București runs daily from approximately 05:00 to 23:00. Exact departure times for every station are available offline in the MetrouBUC app — including underground, without signal.

Which metro app works without internet in Bucharest?

MetrouBUC works 100% offline — map, routes, schedules, GPS tracking and accessibility info are all stored on your phone. No signal or mobile data needed.

How do I travel between major stations in Bucharest by metro?

Use the MetrouBUC route planner: select your departure and destination station, and the app shows the fastest route with all required transfers and estimated time — all offline.

Is there a direct metro line to Bucharest airport?

Not yet. At this time there is no direct metro connection to Henri Coandă International Airport. You can reach the city by train (direct from the airport) or by bus. The MetrouBUC app shows which stations have the closest connections to these services. The future M6 line, which will link the city centre to the airport, is currently under construction and estimated for completion around 2029.
