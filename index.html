What it does

Everything you need
underground

01 🗺️

Smart Route Planning

Find the best path across M1–M5 with automatic transfer optimisation, door-side recommendations, and journey time estimates.

02 📍

GPS Route Tracking

Follow your journey step-by-step in real time. Get notified before transfers and when to get off — even with the screen off.

03 🚪

Door Direction Indicators

Know exactly which side the doors open at every station so you can position yourself before the train arrives.

04 🏢

Station Details

Explore entrances, transport connections, nearby places and points of interest for every station on the network.

05 ⭐

Favourites & Notes

Save your most-used routes for one-tap navigation and attach personal notes to any station — stored only on your device.

06 🕐

Train Timetables

Next departures, first and last train times, peak-hour schedules for weekdays and weekends — all fully offline.

07 🗺️

Interactive Maps

Zoomable SVG metro map plus an OpenStreetMap view with station markers, entrance locations, and nearby POI overlays.

08 🌙

Theme & Language

Light, dark, or system theme. Switch instantly between English, Romanian, French, Italian, German, and Spanish.

09 📵

Works Fully Offline

All route planning, timetables, station data, and journey tracking work without any internet — perfect underground.
